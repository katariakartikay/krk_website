Our Story

Founded in 2009, KRK has established itself as a leading expert in sourcing Home and Lifestyle products from South Asia. The company takes pride in its long-standing relationships with globally recognized brands across Europe, Latin America, Asia, and Australia, serving both retail and wholesale markets.

Under the leadership of Yash Pal Kataria, who brings over 30 years of industry experience, KRK is committed to exceptional standards in quality assurance, ethical compliance, aesthetic innovation, product diversity, and cost-effective production.

Headquartered in New Delhi, KRK specializes in sourcing Home, Lifestyle, and Garment products across South Asia. The company has expanded its reach to include manufacturers in various cities across the region, leveraging deep cultural and market knowledge.
